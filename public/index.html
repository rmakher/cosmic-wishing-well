What is the history of wishing wells?
Wishing wells have existed for thousands of years across cultures worldwide. The ancient Romans tossed coins into wells and springs as offerings to the gods. The Celts believed wells were gateways to the spirit world. In Scandinavian tradition, wells were sacred places where wishes could reach the ears of the gods. The famous Trevi Fountain in Rome receives an estimated €3,000 in coins every day. The Cosmic Wishing Well continues this tradition in the digital age.
